Escape to a place with no boundaries...

...And leave behind an imprisonment of work and stress.

Humans simply weren't meant to be trapped in cubicles, forced into schedules, and subjected to the daily stresses of the capitalist society. As we sit in traffic, we ponder our alternate lives that could have been. It's a fleeting moment for our subjugated imaginations to dissociate from that which slowly eats away at the soul. And every day that passes is another day spent, without having lived. So, are you ready to live like a human?
